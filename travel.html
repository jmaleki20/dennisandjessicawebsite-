Overview

Stonewall Estate is located in Delhi, NY (pronounced del-high) in upstate New York. For guests arriving from out of state, we recommend a combination of flight and rental car.

driving

The distance from New Jersey and New York City (Manhattan) to Stonewall is just under 3 hours away by car. Put the address above into Google Maps or Waze for best results.

Flights

Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR) is the closest major airport with direct flights coming from most of the US. Additional NY-area airports are LaGuardia (LGA) and Kennedy (JFK), but can be more expensive and are further from Stonewall Estate. The drive from EWR to Stonewall is just under 3 hours.

Albany International Airport (ALB) is another option that is closer to Stonewall Estate, but will require a layover. The drive from ALB to Stonewall is just under 2 hours.
To find flights, we recommend using Momondo or Google Flights.

Car Rental

There is plenty of parking on-site at Stonewall Estate and at the AirBnB options provided. For booking a rental car, a good search tool is AutoSlash. Alternatively, those with a Costco membership can use Costco Travel, which generally has the lowest rates.

Since many of our guests are arriving from the Bay Area, let us know if you are looking for a car pool or have extra room in your rental car. We’d love to introduce everyone and encourage carpooling!

Local Dining

We know some of you are foodies and will likely be hungry after making the journey to celebrate with us, so we've put together some popular options in the area for you to get your noms on when you're not at Stonewall.

Breakfast
For breakfast & brunch, there are a few great options in Delhi. Blue Bee Cafe, Bagels N Cream and Cafe On The Green are recommended. Table On Ten is close by and serves up great breakfast pizza.

dining
Dinner options in the area are Greeane Farms, a farm-to-table Mexican Steakhouse restaurant. For those craving sushi, Tuna III is highly rated. There is also Cugino's, a local pizzeria. For burgers & steak, Brushland Eating House is a short drive away.



Local Attractions
Things To Do

The Catskills Mountains has something for everyone, especially during the fall high season. Below are some attractions in the area that we think you'd enjoy.

There are some great breweries and taprooms to visit. Wayside Cider & Taproom, Roscoe Beer Company and Union Grove Distillery are all popular.
For outdoor activities, check out this list of Delhi Hiking Trails, or play a round at Delhi Golf Course.
Right in Delhi, Bushel Collective is a cool storefront and art collective.
If you want to relax and get pampered, check out Spa at The Emerson.
Finally, about an hour away is the National Baseball Hall of Fame, a famous landmark and museum.



FAQs

When time should I get there?
We invite all of our guests to join us for Welcome Drinks at Stonewall on Friday Sep. 13th at 7:30pm. See the Travel page for the full schedule.

What time should I leave?
Stop by Stonewall before your journey home, enjoy some food & say goodbye. We will be serving brunch at 10am on Sunday Sep. 14th to help you work off any hangover.

Is there transportation from the AirBnBs to the venue?
Yes, we will have transportation from the AirBnBs to Stonewall on the Wedding Day, Saturday Sep. 14. Please send us your AirBnB accommodation info as soon you book it, and we will respond with the shuttle schedule.

Are you going to feed me?
Yes. Meals will be served at Stonewall during the following times.
Friday: 7:30pm, Welcome Drinks & Apps
Saturday: Cocktail Hour at 5:00pm, dinner after the ceremony.
Sunday: Breakfast at 10:00am.

What if I have dietary restrictions?
There will be a place to note any dietary restrictions in the RSVP page.

Can I bring my kids?
Children are welcome to participate in daytime activities; however, we cannot provide childcare on-site. Please feel free to reach out to us if you wish to find a local sitter.
