AirBnB Information

We are so grateful that you will be traveling to New York to celebrate with us in September! With that being said, we have provided a list of popular Airbnb’s near the venue that can accommodate individual families and groups of as few as four to as many as eighteen.

The closest hotel is almost an hour away so we encourage you to link up with friends and book your stay as soon as possible to ensure that you don’t miss out on any of these cozy spots. We will be providing transportation from the listed Airbnb’s to the venue, so please be sure to tell us the exact address of the Airbnb of your choice as soon as you book. We can’t wait to celebrate with you all!
