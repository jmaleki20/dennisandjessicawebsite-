Overview

Stonewall Estate is located in Delhi, NY (pronounced del-high) in upstate New York. For guests arriving from out of state, we recommend a combination of flight and rental car.


Flights

Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR) is the closest major airport with direct flights coming from most of the US. Additional NY-area airports are LaGuardia (LGA) and Kennedy (JFK), but can be more expensive and are further from Stonewall Estate. The drive from EWR to Stonewall is just under 3 hours.
Albany International Airport (ALB) is another option that is closer to Stonewall Estate, but will require a layover. The drive from ALB to Stonewall is just under 2 hours.
To compare flight deals, we recommend using momondo.com or google.com/flights
Wedding festivities will begin at X PM on Sept 14, so we recommend arrival on the 13th or early on the 14th. The area is beautiful to explore, and those arriving on the 13th are invited to join us for a Welcome Drinks at Stonewall Estate.


Car Rental

There is plenty of parking on-site at Stonewall Estate and at the AirBnB options provided. For booking a rental car, a good search tool is www.autoslash.com. Alternatively, those with a Costco membership can use Costco Travel (https://www.costcotravel.com/h=4005), which generally has the lowest rates. Since many of our guests are arriving from the Bay Area, let us know if you are looking for a car pool or have extra room in your rental car. We’d love to introduce everyone and encourage carpooling!

AIRBNB

We are so grateful that you will be traveling to New York to celebrate with us in September! Below you'll find a list of popular AirBnB homes near the venue that can accommodate individual families and groups of as few as four to as many as eighteen.

The closest hotel is almost an hour away so we encourage you to link up with friends and book your stay as soon as possible to ensure that you don’t miss out on any of these cozy spots. We will be providing transportation from the listed Airbnb’s to the venue, so please be sure to tell us the exact address of the Airbnb of your choice as soon as you book. We can’t wait to celebrate with you all!
