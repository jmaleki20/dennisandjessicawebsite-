Overview

Stonewall Estate is located in Delhi, NY (pronounced del-high) in upstate New York. For guests arriving from out of state, we recommend a combination of flight and rental car.

Map to Venue launch


driving

The distance from New Jersey and New York City (Manhattan) to Stonewall is just under 3 hours away by car. Put the address above into Google Maps or Waze for best results.


Flights

Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR) is the closest major airport with direct flights coming from most of the US. Additional NY-area airports are LaGuardia (LGA) and Kennedy (JFK), but can be more expensive and are further from Stonewall Estate. The drive from EWR to Stonewall is just under 3 hours.

Albany International Airport (ALB) is another option that is closer to Stonewall Estate, but will require a layover. The drive from ALB to Stonewall is just under 2 hours.
To find flights, we recommend using Momondo or Google Flights.

Please see the Events page for schedule information to plan your arrival. The area is beautiful to explore, and we recommend arriving earlier on Friday Sep 13th to leave ample time for travel to Stonewall.

Car Rental

There is plenty of parking on-site at Stonewall Estate and at the AirBnB options provided. For booking a rental car, a good search tool is AutoSlash. Alternatively, those with a Costco membership can use Costco Travel, which generally has the lowest rates.

Since many of our guests are arriving from the Bay Area, let us know if you are looking for a car pool or have extra room in your rental car. We’d love to introduce everyone and encourage carpooling!

Local Dining

We know some of you are foodies and will likely be hungry after making the journey to celebrate with us, so we've put together some popular options in the area for you to get your noms on when you're not at Stonewall.

Breakfast
For breakfast & brunch, there are a few great options in Delhi. Blue Bee Cafe, Bagels N Cream and Cafe On The Green are recommended. Table On Ten is close by and serves up great breakfast pizza. The Yellow Deli claimed to be the best food in town.


dining
Dinner options in the area are Greeane Farms, a farm-to-table Mexican Steakhouse restaurant. For those craving sushi, Tuna III is highly rated. There is also Cugino's, a local pizzeria or Alfrescos for some yummy italian food. For burgers & steak, Brushland Eating House or Autumn Cafe.

Local Attractions

The Catskills Mountains has something for everyone, especially during the gorgeous fall season! Below are some attractions in the area that we think you would enjoy.

wine & beer
There are some great breweries and taprooms to visit. Wayside Cider & Taproom, Roscoe Beer Company, Bear Pond Winery and Union Grove Distillery are all popular.
outdoor
For outdoor activities, check out table rock or this list of Delhi Hiking Trails, or play a round at Delhi Golf Course. Right in Delhi, Bushel Collective is a cool storefront and art collective.
misc.
If you want to relax and get pampered, check out Spa at The Emerson.
Finally, about an hour away is the National Baseball Hall of Fame, a famous landmark and museum.
