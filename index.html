How We Met

09.27.14

Jessica and Dennis met when working at Uber, and while Dennis admits that he remembers seeing Jessica around the office, it took a little while for them to get introduced. As luck would have it, a mutual co-worker and part-time matchmaker organized a hike and invited them both. From there, they became friends, confided in each other, and grew closer. After a year of friendship, they decided to make things official and became a couple. And the rest is history.

The Proposal

04.08.18

Dennis invited Jessica to brunch in San Francisco at one of the first brunch places they visited together. He strategically had them park at one of their favorite and most picturesque spots in San Francisco - the Palace of Fine Arts. Walking through the park on a planned path, they got to a spot overlooking the lake, and Dennis romatically (and maybe awkwardly) got on one knee and proposed to Jessica, for them to spend the rest of their lives together in marriage. Jessica was shocked, and after a few moments remembered to say 'Yes!'.

We're so excited to celebrate this next chapter in our lives with you.

See you in Delhi, New York!
